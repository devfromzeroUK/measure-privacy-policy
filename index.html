We Do Not Collect Personal Information

Direct Collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users. Our app is designed to offer its services without requiring any personal data input or collection.

This data processing by Google AdMob is subject to Google's Privacy Policy, which you can review here.

By Us: We do not share any personal information because we do not collect it.

While we do not collect personal data, we take reasonable steps to ensure the security of the app and to prevent unauthorized access to or misuse of information by third parties like Google AdMob.

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 16. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 16, please contact us immediately, so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If Google AdMob has collected data via our app, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request access to the data Google AdMob holds about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Ask for incorrect data to be corrected.
  • Right to Erasure: Request that your data be deleted (also known as the right to be forgotten).
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Ask to restrict the processing of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for direct marketing or for reasons connected with your particular situation.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time for personalized advertising by Google AdMob, although this will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

To exercise these rights, you would need to contact Google directly through the mechanisms provided in their privacy policy or contact us, and we will guide you to the appropriate resources.

8. Data Transfers Outside the EEA

Google AdMob might transfer your data to servers located outside the EEA. Google complies with the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework and uses Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure an adequate level of data protection.

9. Privacy by Design

Our app is designed to minimize data collection and processing, adhering to the principles of privacy by design.

10. Data Breach Notification
